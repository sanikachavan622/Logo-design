# Brew Leaf Café Logo Design

## Objective
The objective of this project was to design a clean and memorable logo for “Brew Leaf Café,” a café brand that combines the essence of herbal wellness with traditional café culture. The logo was created to represent freshness, comfort, sustainability, and a premium beverage experience.

---

## Task Overview
The logo design combines a coffee cup with fresh green leaves to visually represent herbal drinks, tea, and coffee culture. The design uses earthy tones and elegant typography to create a calm, natural, and welcoming brand identity suitable for a modern café.

---

## Tools Used
- Adobe Illustrator

---

## About Logo
- Coffee cup representing café culture
- Green leaves symbolizing freshness and herbal ingredients
- Curved shapes creating a smooth and organic appearance

---

## Brand Concept
The Brew Leaf Café logo reflects:
- Natural and plant-based ingredients
- Herbal wellness and freshness
- Sustainability and comfort
- Premium beverage experience

---

## Steps Performed
1. Created initial logo concepts and sketches
2. Combined coffee cup and leaf elements
3. Selected earthy and natural color palette
4. Applied elegant and legible typography
5. Refined logo layout and proportions
6. Exported final logo variations

---

## Outcome
Successfully designed a professional café logo that visually communicates freshness, warmth, and modern café branding while maintaining simplicity and elegance.
